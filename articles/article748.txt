<p>On Tuesday, June 21, officials from the White House Gender Policy Council and National Security Council convened a meeting with leaders and experts on global reproductive rights. Participants discussed how a ruling in <em>Dobbs v. Jackson Women’s Health Organization</em> might impact access to sexual and reproductive health and rights around the world, and offered recommendations to the Administration to protect and advance women’s health globally.   </p>
<p>White House officials thanked the leaders for their efforts and underscored the Administration’s commitment to support sexual and reproductive health and rights across the globe.</p>
